Fillet Steak

Description

Another one of our classic meal that we love to share.

This meal is so good, if you stare too long you may fall in love.

Ingredients

  • Peas
  • Potatoes
  • 2 large shallots
  • Butter
  • 100ml red wine
  • Rump Steak
  • double cream
  • brandy
  • green peppercorns

Instructions

  1. Boil peas in hot water for about 5 minutes.
  2. Since the fillet is quite a thick cut of meat for medium rare it will take about 4-5 minutes on both sides.
  3. Coat well with butter until its cooked.
  4. Peel the potato and cut into rectangular pieces.
  5. Cook the potatoes until golden brown, around 8-10 minutes.

Making Peppercorn sauce

  1. Put butter in a sauce pan, then fry the diced shallots and add brandy until its almost all reduced.
  2. Pour 100ml of wine.
  3. Bring it to a boil and wait 5 minutes.
  4. Finally pour the double cream and let it thicken to your liking.
And of course, enjoy :).
