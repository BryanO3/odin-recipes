Rump Steak

Description

A lovely easy meal that any family will enjoy to ahve on their plate.

Fresh and vibrant, oozing with such flavours that explodes as soon as you bite into it.

Ingredients

  • Rump steak
  • Baby potatoes
  • mushrooms
  • Wild Rockets
  • Butter

Instructions

  1. Cut potatoes in half and boil for about 25 minutes
  2. Fry the mushrooms, and drop some butter. Wait untill its fully caramelised and take off pan
  3. Cook the rump steak on a hot pan, about 3 minutes on each side for a perfect medium rare
  4. Drop some butter and coat the steak well, until its done cooking
  5. finally garnish with a side of wild rockets and voila
